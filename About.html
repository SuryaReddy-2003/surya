Ice cream is a beloved frozen dessert made by churning a mixture of milk or cream, sweeteners,
and flavorings while simultaneously freezing it.
Here are some key points about ice cream:
1.It is made from cream or butterfat, milk, sugar, and flavorings.
2.Popular flavors include vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry.
3.Ice cream has been enjoyed for centuries.
4.Ingredients can vary, but typically include milk or
cream, sweeteners, and flavoring,Good teats.
